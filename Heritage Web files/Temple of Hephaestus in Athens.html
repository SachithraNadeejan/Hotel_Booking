Temple of Hephaestus in Athens

The Temple of Hephaestus, also known as Hephaisteion or earlier as the Theseion, is a well-preserved ancient Greek temple. It is located on the western side of the Agora of Athens, atop the Agoraios Kolonos hill.

Discover the architectural beauty and historical significance of this temple dedicated to Hephaestus, the ancient Greek god of blacksmiths, craftsmen, and artisans.
